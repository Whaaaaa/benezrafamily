This week's Parsha contains the tragic deaths of Nadav and Avihu, and their father Aharon's reaction to hearing the news. Rashi explains Aharon's silence in the face of unbearable loss -- and from that silence, the sages derive an entire framework for how we, too, must hold grief and faith together without either one erasing the other.

Good Shabbos!
