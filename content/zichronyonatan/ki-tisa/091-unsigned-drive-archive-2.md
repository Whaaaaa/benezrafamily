The Eternal One spoke to Moses, saying: "When you take a census of the Israelite men according to their army enrollment, each shall pay the Eternal a ransom for himself on being enrolled, that no plague may come upon them through their being enrolled." - Exodus 30:11-12

What is the connection between being counted and being spared from plague?

What is the connection between the counting people and plague

Rashi says it is the evil eye:

"For numbers (i. e. things that have been numbered) are subject to the influence of the "evil eye", and therefore if you count them by their polls pestilence may befall them, as we find happened, in the days of David (II Samuel 24:10 and 15).

Explain a bit What they mean is, ...

These sources make it clear that purposeless enumeration of people purley for pride is a prescription for plague.

This connection is incredibly timely. We are amidst a wave of unprecedented pestilence that happens to coincide with the beginning of the American census. Now seems to be an opportune time to reflect on the importance of ensuring the enumeration of people is a constructive endeavor and to embrace humility.

What would be destructive?

What would be constructive?

Coverage of the Coronavirus has inundated us with statistics, mortality rates, death counts, the total number of the infected. These counts are the polar opposite of the unnecessary and self-gratifying censuses we have been warned against. Lets be sure to use these numbers to do good!
