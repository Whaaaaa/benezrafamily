Happy Chanukah to all! What a beautiful time of year. The festival of lights has illuminated every Jewish home for millennia, and as the days pass and the candles multiply, we are reminded that even the smallest quantity of pure light can overcome vast darkness -- a fitting theme as Miketz carries Yosef from the darkness of an Egyptian dungeon into the light of the palace, on the strength of a single gift: his ability to interpret dreams truthfully, even to a king.

Chag Urim Sameach!
